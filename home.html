Shantabai Overview

Overview

Shantabai is an on-demand platform that helps users find quick, reliable, and nearby household help. Designed for those who need short-term assistance, Shantabai provides a seamless experience for finding maids to handle tasks such as cleaning, cooking, and more, for as short as an hour or as long as needed.

01

Real-Time Booking: Users can instantly find and book the nearest available maid based on their location, task, and time requirement.

02

Flexible Duration: Services can be booked for short-term tasks (an hour or two) or for longer durations as per the user's needs.

03

Transparent Pricing: Clear and upfront pricing options allow users to choose services that fit their budget.

04

Secure Payments: Integrated payment options for a seamless, cashless experience.
